It's In The Game!

For this project, we have analyzed data scraped from https://www.wholefoodsmarket.com/. Our Product page has information about Whole Foods products and nutrition facts to bettern inform consumers.

Our Goal.

To better inform consumers of the products that they use. Most of the times it can be a challenge to read nutritional information about the products you may purchase online. Our website allows you to navigate through Whole Foods products and look at the nutritional values that each product contains.
